Grave - can you escape?

A second ago, you could feel a light draft of cold air from up the stairs you just descend. But with a thudding noise - stillness. No air.

You are locked in this grave.

Will it be yours?

Use the ARROW KEYS to move around the tomb.
Use ESC to Pause / Unpause the game.
Use E to use buttons, levers, what have you.
Use Enter to finally unlock the door.
Avoid the ghouls and skeletons and whatnot, find the key and find the exit.
